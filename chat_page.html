This is the screen of overall chat page. Users are able to

1) access to personal task interface and documents

2) access to contacts

3) search, view


Users can  create a new study groups. User will enter information about:

 1) their groups including group name,

 2) description (specify purpose and activities as to remind members of their learning goals and provide information for potential members),   

3) community where their group belong to( easy for potential members to navigate groups)

4) group duration (remind users to focus on their study deadlines and avoid the group becoming an chat group for pure social purpose),

5) entry requirements(filter and locate members with common learning goals)

6) privacy(determine whether the group will be found by search)


After the completion of group information, users can click next button to go to the chat page
